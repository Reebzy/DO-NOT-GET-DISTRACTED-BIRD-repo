## Full description

DONT GET DISTRACTED BIRD is a focus enforcement tool for people who know they're prone to distraction and want their browser to fight back on their behalf.

**It's not a wellness app. It's a guard.**

When Focus Mode is on, a stern heraldic cardinal watches your tabs and intercepts accidental navigation. If you stray, it catches you — and makes you choose deliberately before you go.

---

**What it does:**

🔴 **Focus Mode toggle** — one click to activate. Your current tab is automatically assigned as your first focus tab. Everything else is under watch.

🔴 **Tab interstitial** — switch to a non-focus tab and a full-screen countdown appears. You have 3–10 seconds to go back, add the tab to focus, or admit defeat. The bird waits.

🔴 **URL navigation lock** — navigate off a whitelisted domain and the same countdown fires. Accidentally followed a link to Reddit? The bird noticed.

🔴 **Window focus detection** — leave the Chrome window and your tab title starts flashing. An OS notification fires: "Come back." When you return, it tells you how long you were gone.

🔴 **Hotkey lockdown** — Ctrl+T, Ctrl+Tab, Ctrl+N, and direct tab jumps are suppressed while focus mode is active.

🔴 **Session log** — every distraction event is logged. At the end of a session, you'll know exactly how often the bird had to intervene.

🔴 **Domain whitelist** — pre-add the sites you know you'll need. First navigation? Already allowed. Linear, Figma, GitHub? Not a problem.

---

**Three choices when you stray:**

← GO BACK — immediate return, no questions.
Add to Focus — if you actually need the tab, add it.
I AM DISTRACTED. END FOCUS MODE. — the honest option.

---

**Core philosophy: friction, not blockade.**

You can always get past it. The countdown and the deliberate button press are the friction — not a wall. The bird can't stop you. But it makes you own the choice.

---

**Permissions used:**

- `tabs` — monitor which tab is active to enforce focus
- `notifications` — OS alert when you leave the focus window
- `storage` — save your whitelist and settings between sessions
- `scripting` — inject the focus guard and overlays into pages
- `activeTab` — read your current tab when you toggle Focus Mode
- `webNavigation` — detect URL changes inside focus tabs

No data leaves your browser. No accounts. No analytics. No tracking.
