DONT GET DISTRACTED BIRD — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Summary

This extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data outside of your own browser. Everything stays on your device.

Data we collect

We collect nothing. The extension stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in storage APIs:

  • Your domain whitelist (domains you've chosen to allow during focus sessions)
  • Your settings (countdown duration preference)
  • Your session log (focus events from the current browser session — cleared on browser restart)

This data never leaves your browser. It is not sent to any server, third party, or external service.

Data we do not collect

  • No browsing history
  • No tab contents or URLs beyond what's needed to enforce focus mode in real time
  • No personal information
  • No usage analytics
  • No crash reports transmitted externally

Third-party services

None. The extension uses no external APIs, analytics platforms, tracking pixels, or remote services of any kind.

Permissions explained

  • tabs — Required to monitor which tab is active so the extension can enforce focus mode rules.
  • notifications — Required to send an OS-level alert when you leave your focus window.
  • storage — Required to save your whitelist and settings locally between browser sessions.
  • scripting — Required to inject the focus interstitial and return overlay into web pages.
  • activeTab — Required to read your current tab's URL when you toggle Focus Mode on.
  • webNavigation — Required to detect when a focus tab navigates to an off-whitelist domain.
